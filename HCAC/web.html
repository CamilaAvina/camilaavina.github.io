The mission of the Hispanic Community Affairs Council (HCAC) is to promote the value of education, cultural diversity, and community involvement.

HCAC strives to increase the number of Latinos who successfully graduate from college by awarding scholarships to academically-talented and highly-motivated students in need of financial assistance.
