Welcome to Tommy's World

There is a world that not many people understand. We come from different places. We feel different things. Only the people who are in this world will understand why it is like this where there are some things and not others. People need to figure out why there is so much chaos in this world. We venture off without knowing about the world. Where souls are lost in the abyss and can never get out. There is madness in this world that no one can escape. To enter this world and become part of this world they need to fugure out how the riddle is made to stop this madness.
